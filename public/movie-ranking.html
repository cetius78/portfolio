My Favorite Movies

The Breakfast Club

The nostalgia is strong on this one, but it is one of the few movies that I can truly say influenced me in (mostly) positive ways.

The Princess Bride

There's a shortage of perfect movies in this world--this is one of them.

The Rocky Horror Picture Show

In this case, it's less about the movie and more about its affect on my social life and college experience.
